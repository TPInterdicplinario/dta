Our process

The process of production of biopolymers from polyhydroxyalkanoates (PHA's) has gained great importance among researchers and industrial biotechnology thanks to recent studies that promise to be a great alternative to replace conventional polymers. This process consists of three major global sections: Sterilization, Fermentation and Separation.
